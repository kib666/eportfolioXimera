About Ximera Business Solution Inc.

Ximera Business Solution Inc. is a Philippine-based technology and business services company that provides integrated solutions spanning IT infrastructure management, enterprise resource planning, and multi-platform e-commerce operations. The company serves a range of clients across industries, offering technical support, system administration, and digital commerce management as its core service lines.

The company operates its own e-commerce platform, watershop.ph, and maintains active product listings across major Philippine marketplaces including Shopee, Lazada, and Shopify. Internally, Ximera utilizes enterprise software such as Amici Businessware for financial management and operations, and OTOBO for IT ticket management and support tracking.

Its IT department maintains server infrastructure including Linux-based systems, remote access platforms (AnyDesk, AWS Cebu, X2Go), and VPN solutions for secure connectivity. The company also deploys on-site technical support services to client organizations such as Thermovar Accounting and Mayon Corp.

Background of the Training Project Involvement

A description of the projects each intern was involved in, their objectives, and individual responsibilities.

Fig 1.0 – Mendevil Conducting Hardware Diagnostics

System Administrator Support — Hardware, Servers & Support Tickets

Project Overview: Mendevil was assigned under the System Administrator role at Ximera Business Solution Inc. His project involvement centered on maintaining, diagnosing, and deploying IT infrastructure across the company and its client sites. This aligned directly with Ximera's goal of providing reliable IT support services to internal teams and external clients.

Main Objectives: The primary objectives were to ensure operational continuity of hardware and software systems, support server deployment for remote locations (including Zamboanga), maintain IT support ticket queues via OTOBO, and implement security infrastructure such as OpenVPN across Windows and Ubuntu systems.

Specific Responsibilities:

  • Diagnosing hardware units including UPS devices, printers (Brother DCP-T420W, Brother HL-1210W), and computers — documenting findings in structured diagnostic reports
  • Configuring and deploying server setups through Bash scripting on Linux systems — installing MariaDB, X2Go, Amici Businessware, and Wine
  • Performing remote diagnostics via AWS Cebu and AnyDesk
  • Managing OTOBO support tickets — reviewing, documenting work notes, sending stakeholder email updates, and closing resolved tickets
  • Implementing OpenVPN on Windows and Ubuntu systems for multiple staff members
  • Reformatting and reimaging laptops for donation and redeployment
  • On-site network installations at Thermovar Accounting and hardware support at Mayon Corp
  • Network cable crimping, router installation, and workstation redeployment
  • Setting up development environments (VS Code with Flutter and Dart extensions)
Fig 1.1 – Caya Analyzing Cross-Platform Product Data

E-Commerce / Logistics — Multi-Platform Product Management

Project Overview: Caya was assigned to the e-commerce logistics department, where his primary project involved managing and synchronizing product data across Ximera's multi-platform online retail ecosystem spanning BusinessWare, Lazada, Shopee, and Shopify (watershop.ph). This aligned with Ximera's business goal of maintaining accurate and competitive product listings across all active sales channels.

Main Objectives: The objectives were to migrate all Lazada product listings to Shopee and Shopify, maintain data consistency across platforms, improve product organization on Shopify, and conduct QA testing of the Shopify checkout process.

Specific Responsibilities:

  • Studying and analyzing product data flows between BusinessWare, Lazada, Shopee, and Shopify — identifying discrepancies in stock quantities, pricing, and listings
  • Using the browser Inspect tool to extract structured product data from Shopee and converting it to formatted Excel files using AI tools
  • Designing and maintaining a multi-sheet Excel workbook tracking product names, SKUs, pricing, upload status, and markup calculations
  • Migrating all Lazada products to Shopee and Shopify while maintaining accurate tracking records
  • Creating new product collection categories on Shopify (Bathroom Shower, Bathroom Faucet) to improve navigation
  • Writing product titles and descriptions for Shopify listings aligned with watershop.ph branding standards
  • Conducting QA testing of the Shopify checkout process, email notifications, and form validation
  • Post-upload verification of all listings to confirm accurate pricing, stock, and product details
Fig 1.2 – Placencia Conducting Inventory and Expense Analysis

E-Commerce / Logistics & Financial Encoding — Inventory and Expense Management

Project Overview: Placencia was initially assigned to the logistics section, focusing on product management across BusinessWare, Shopify, Shopee, and Lazada. In the later phase of her internship, she transitioned to expense encoding in Amici Businessware under Ma'am Gemma's supervision. Both assignments supported Ximera's goals of accurate inventory management and financial transparency.

Main Objectives: The objectives were to ensure Shopee product listings accurately reflected BusinessWare inventory and pricing, maintain a structured upload workflow with 1.50 markup uniformly applied, and later, accurately encode months of expense data into Amici Businessware to support financial accountability.

Specific Responsibilities:

  • Gathering stock and pricing data from BusinessWare, verifying entries against Shopee listings, and flagging items requiring correction (missing SKUs, unlisted prices, unavailable listings)
  • Designing a color-coded tracking system in Excel — red for items needing correction, green for successfully updated entries
  • Batch uploading updated products to Shopee with a 1.50 markup formula applied uniformly
  • Transitioning to expense encoding in Amici Businessware: creating party entries, generating receipts, applying color-coded categorization, encoding day-by-day expense records
  • Reviewing completed expense records for accuracy before finalization — cross-checking receipt totals against source documents
  • Reporting discrepancies in party records to supervisors rather than making unilateral corrections
  • Logging extended hours during high-demand periods to complete assigned encoding tasks on time
